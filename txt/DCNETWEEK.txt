         TITLE 'NETWEEK'                                                        
Module deduces Netpak pocket-piece week number from selected date               
                                                                                
Parameter 1                  A(6-byte EBCDIC date)                              
                                                                                
Parameter 2                  A(GETDAY)                                          
                                                                                
                                                                                
Parameter 3                  A(ADDAY)                                           
                                                                                
The week number will be passed back in the first byte of Parameter 1.           
The year number will be passed back in the first byte of Parameter 2.           
                                                                                
On-line version is core-resident phase TOOA17.
