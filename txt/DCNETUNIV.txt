         TITLE 'NETUNIV'                                                        
Module derives network universes for specified year.                            
                                                                                
Parameter 1    byte  1       Year number                                        
               bytes 2-4     A(work code)                                       
                                                                                
Module will fill in all universes from PWUK611 to PWUHOME inclusive.  Universes 
are in hundreds.                                                                
                                                                                
On-line, core-resident version is T00A16.
