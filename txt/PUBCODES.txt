User code assignments for Client Service/Publications                           
                                                                                
1st digit - 5                                                                   
                                                                                
2nd digit - TYPE                                                                
            0 - Unassigned                                                      
            1 - CS/admin                                                        
            4 - Service notice                                                  
            5 - Publication                                                     
            6 - Update to pub                                                   
            8 - Misc                                                            
            9 - Assembler program                                               
                                                                                
3rd digit - SYSTEM                                                              
            0 - Unassigned                                                      
            1 - Gen DDS                                                         
            2 - Spotpak                                                         
            3 - Printpak                                                        
            4 - Netpak                                                          
            5 - Prodpak                                                         
            6 - Accpak                                                          
            7 - Reppak                                                          
            8 - Internal                                                        
            9 - Other                                                           
                                                                                
4th digit - STATUS                                                              
            0 - Unassigned                                                      
            1 - Current                                                         
            2 - Draft                                                           
            3 - Historical
