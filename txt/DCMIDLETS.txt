         TITLE 'MIDLETS'                                                        
Allows printing of a series of words which are expanded into mid (8x8) letters. 
Each data card may have up to 13 characters.                                    
                                                                                
     // JOB MIDDLING // EXEC MIDLETS data card data card                        
                                                                                
     REVERSE=mask            Causes subsequent data cards to print in           
                             white against a background of the mask             
     data card               which is up to 70 characters.
