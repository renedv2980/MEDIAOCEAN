         TITLE 'GETCOMM (UK)'                                                   
Fills in commission rate in a buy record.                                       
                                                                                
Parameter 1    bytes 0-3     A(composite profile)                               
                                                                                
Parameter 2    bytes 0-3     A(buy record)                                      
                                                                                
If profile is not recognised, commission rate is set to zero.
