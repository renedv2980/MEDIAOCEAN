         TITLE 'REQUS'                                                          
Macro generates a standard set of register equates.  It can be called more than 
once within an assembly but only the first call will generate the equates.      
                                                                                
         REQUS                                                                  
                                                                                
General registers 0-9   equate to R0-R9                                         
General registers 10-15 equate to RA-RF
