         TITLE 'GETUNIV (UK)'                                                   
Module builds universe record from area universe records on MEDFILE.            
                                                                                
Parameter 1    byte  0       If non zero read for and apply factor record with  
                               this code.                                       
               bytes 1-3     A(universe record)                                 
                                                                                
Parameter 2    bytes 0-3     A(DATAMGR)
