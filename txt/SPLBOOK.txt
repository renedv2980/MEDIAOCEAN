                      MARKET. AS ON THE SPL SCREEN IN THE CONTRACT PRO-

                      IS ACCEPTABLE. THE USER HAS ALSO CHOSEN TO OVERRIDE       
                      THE CURRENT MONTH ON THE THIRD CONTRACT
