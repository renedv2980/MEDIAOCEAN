         TITLE 'LOZENGE'                                                        
Module will receive up to 8 characters, will center them, expand them to big    
letters and print on SYSLST.                                                    
                                                                                
Parameter 1                  A(8-byte field)                                    
                                                                                
Any letter or number may be included in the 8-byte field.                       
                                                                                
LOZENGE calls PRINT with a V-type, so PRINT will be auto-linked.
