         TITLE 'TMUNPK (UK)'                                                    
Converts 4-byte to variable time.                                               
                                                                                
Parameter 1    bytes 0-3     A(input time)                                      
                                                                                
Parameter 2    bytes 0-3     A(9-byte output area) - pre-cleared to spaces      
                                                                                
Parameter 3    bytes 0-3     Set to length of output.
