         TITLE 'GETBUDG (UK)'                                                   
Module builds a table of area/category budgets for a burst period.  Include     
MEGETBUDGD for table DSECT.                                                     
                                                                                
Parameter 1    bytes 0-3     A(campaign record)                                 
                                                                                
Parameter 2    bytes 0-3     A(burst record)                                    
                                                                                
Parameter 3    bytes 0-3     A(budget table)                                    
                                                                                
Parameter 4    bytes 0-3     A(station list) (TV & radio only)
