         TITLE 'DELEL'                                                          
Removes elements with a code of X'FF' from a record.                            
                                                                                
Parameter 1                  A(record)                                          
                                                                                
After DELEL, Parameter 1 will contain the new length of the record.             
                                                                                
Note - For DELEL with account file, include ACDELEL.
