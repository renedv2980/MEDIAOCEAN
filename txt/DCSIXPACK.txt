         TITLE 'SIXPACK'                                                        
Conversion module support six-bit format.                                       
                                                                                
Parameters     SIXPACK                                                          
                                                                                
     1         A(EBCDIC string)      (A-Z, 0-9, space only)                     
                                                                                
     2         A(output area)                                                   
                                                                                
     3         Length of input                                                  
               (s/b multiple of 4)
