         TITLE 'SPROG'                                                          
Conditional spec macro.                                                         
                                                                                
(Name)   SPROG n1(,n2...,nn)                                                    
(Name)   SPROG n1,THRU,n2                                                       
                                                                                
Means that following specs apply to subprograms n1(,n2...,nn) or, with the THRU 
feature, n1 thru n2.                                                            
                                                                                
There is a field RCSUBPRG in the WORKD which is tested.
