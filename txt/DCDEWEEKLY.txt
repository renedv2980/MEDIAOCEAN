Weekly Metered Market Data                                                      
                                                                                
Homes figures by week for NSI metered markets. The weeks are numbered based on a
Monday calendar date. They run from 1-53 sequentially. Data is given for each   
week and a four week moving average. The individual week data has the typical   
bit turned on so that DEGET can access it. This data is loaded to the file under
media "W".
