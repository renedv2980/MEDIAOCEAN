         TITLE 'UPOUT'                                                          
Module edits an upgrade expression.                                             
                                                                                
Parameter 1                  A(upgrade element)                                 
                                                                                
Parameter 2                  A(output area)                                     
                                                                                
After UPOUT, Parameter 2 byte 1 will contain the length of the output           
expression.  This may be zero.
