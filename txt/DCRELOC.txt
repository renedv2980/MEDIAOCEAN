         TITLE 'RELOC'                                                          
Macro will generate code to return program relocation factor.                   
                                                                                
 Formats                                                                        
 -------                                                                        
                                                                                
         RELOC                    Relocation factor in RE.                      
                                                                                
         RELOC (Rx)               Relocation factor in Rx.                      
                                                                                
         RELOC name               Relocation factor in RE and stored at name.
