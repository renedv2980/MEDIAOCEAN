         TITLE 'DTPACK'                                                         
Converts 6-byte EBCDIC dates to 2-byte special format:                          
                                                                                
Bits 0-6=year, 7-10=month, 11-15=day                                            
                                                                                
Parameter 1                  A(6-byte date)                                     
Parameter 2                  A(2-byte date)
